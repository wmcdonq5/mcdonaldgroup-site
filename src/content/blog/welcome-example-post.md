This is an example blog post created while setting up your content manager. You can edit this post or delete it entirely from the admin panel at **/admin** — log in, click "Blog Posts," and select this entry.

To write a new post: log into `/admin`, click "New Blog Posts," fill in a title, date, short description, and the body text, then click **Publish**. Your site will automatically rebuild and the new post will appear on the [Blog page](/blog/) within a minute or two.
