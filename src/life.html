Most people don’t need just any life insurance — they need the right coverage at the best price. As your broker, we work for you, not the insurance companies, searching plans from a variety of insurers to find the best fit.

Protect your loved ones financially, no matter what

Life insurance isn’t just a policy — it’s peace of mind. If you were to pass away unexpectedly, your loved ones wouldn’t be left with financial burdens. A fixed sum is paid directly to the beneficiaries you choose — your spouse, children, or whoever you want to support.

Think of it as a safety net for your family. They can use the payout to cover expenses like:

  • Mortgage payments
  • Daily living costs
  • College tuition
  • Outstanding debts

Life insurance ensures your loved ones are taken care of, even when you’re gone.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need life insurance?
If anyone depends on your income or would face financial hardship if you were gone — a spouse, children, or aging parents — life insurance is worth considering.
What are the different types of life insurance?
Term life covers you for a set period (e.g., 10–30 years) at a lower cost. Permanent life (whole, universal, variable) lasts your entire life and can build cash value. We’ll help you decide which fits your goals.
How much life insurance coverage do I need?
A common starting point is 10–15 times your annual income, adjusted for debts, future expenses like college, and existing savings. We can run the numbers with you.
What factors affect the cost of life insurance?
Age, health, tobacco use, coverage amount, and policy type all affect your premium. Locking in coverage earlier and in good health typically means a lower rate.
Can I get life insurance without a medical exam?
Yes — many carriers offer simplified-issue or guaranteed-issue policies that skip the medical exam, though they may cost more or cap coverage amounts.
Can I get life insurance if I have a pre-existing health condition?
Often, yes. Pricing and options vary by carrier and condition, which is exactly why comparing multiple insurers matters — we shop the market to find one that will work with your health history.
