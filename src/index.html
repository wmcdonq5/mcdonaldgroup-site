FAQ

Frequently asked questions

I’m overwhelmed by all the insurance options. Can you help?
Absolutely — that’s exactly what we’re here for. In a free, no-obligation consultation, we’ll walk through your situation, explain your options in plain language, and narrow the field down to plans that actually fit your needs and budget.
What happens if I need to file a claim?
You’re not on your own. As your broker, we act as your advocate with the carrier — helping you understand the process, gather what’s needed, and follow up until it’s resolved.
Beyond finding insurance, can you help with anything else?
Yes. We provide annual plan reviews, help you navigate life changes (new job, moving, turning 65), and keep you informed about enrollment periods and plan changes year to year.
How do I get started with McDonald Group?
Call us at 229-457-6943 or fill out the short form on our Contact page. We’ll schedule a free consultation at a time that works for you.
Why should I use an insurance broker instead of going directly to an insurance company?
A broker works for you, not one company. We compare plans across many carriers to find your best fit, and there’s no added cost to you — broker compensation comes from the carrier, not your pocket.
